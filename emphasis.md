# Emphasis

The underscore key ( _ ) and asterisk key ( * ) are necessary for **boldening** and _italicizing_ text. 

## How to **bolden**:

Double asterisks and double underlines before and after your text will **bolden** it.

` **This will bolden your text** `

`__This will also bolden your text__ `

## How to *italicize*:

Single asterisks and single underlines before and after your text will *italicize* it.

` *This will italicize your text* `

` _This will also italicize your text_ `
